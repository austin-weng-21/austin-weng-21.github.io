Foundations Reflection

Core Learning

There are three new things that I learned form the last five weeks core learning. The first and most important one for me is the emotional intelligence. Although I thought I knew this EQ thing very well but I never actually used it well until this IT learning journey. During the last five weeks I came across a lot of problems such as totally new things to learn, balancing family time and learning and my company stuff. It sounds easier than it actually is. Until now I finally realize EQ plays such an important role in our life. The second thing that I learned is that our neuro and brain can reshape physically. Before I thought that all learning were just happening at our mental level. The third thing is the listening and looping. Fully listening and no interruption actually gives me the full information of other people. This is really important for teamworks.
